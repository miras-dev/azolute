About service

Our Approach
to Web Design

At Azolute, we build websites that are fast, conversion-focused, and designed to establish trust with your audience. Every site we deliver is the starting point for a longer automation relationship — built at competitive prices so you can get started without barriers.

View works

Discovery & Strategy

+
-

We start every project by understanding your business goals, target audience, and key messaging. This ensures the website we build aligns with how you want to be perceived and what action you want visitors to take.

Design & Development

+
-

We design clean, modern websites using Webflow, Relume, or HTML/CSS/JS depending on your needs. Every site is mobile-responsive, fast-loading, and built with conversion in mind — guiding visitors towards taking action.

AI Chatbot Integration

+
-

Every website we build includes a custom AI chatbot. It uses your business documents or website data as a knowledge base (RAG) to answer visitor questions, and it collects leads — name, email, and phone — forwarding them directly to you so you never miss an enquiry.

Delivery & Launch

+
-

After review and final approval, we handle deployment and launch. We provide a handover with everything you need to manage the site and continue communication to ensure you’re fully satisfied with the result.

Ongoing Automation

+
-

After delivery, we stay in contact and identify opportunities to further automate your operations — from email workflows and CRM integration to AI-based task handling. The website is just the beginning of a long-term partnership.
